Learning-Curve Games

This section is for some games I make with free assets via unity with courses while trying to learn fundamentals. These games do not provide any vision of mine but I will put them here anyway to show the understatement of my unity level and systems per game.


Note: Be aware I make these games just to learn unity so many of these games are unpolished or do not have a proper ending as i make them to learn unity. I don't plan to polish them much or release them for the future, they are just here to show my learning level of unity so please be aware of it before judging.

DriverProject

DriverProject is the first and most fundamental game I make. I learn the most starting level knowledge from it, the objective is collecting ice creams and giving them to customers. It has no end but there are a total 16 customers you can count yourself and win if you help all of them.


Download: Mega Mediafire

SnowBoardProject

SnowBoardProject is a project I have the most fun making and where I learn most coding fundamentals. It has an end if you win or crash. The main objective is flipping and collecting as many points as you can do!


Download: Mega Mediafire
